Safety for All

Protecting communities

A positive influence

There’s a strong correlation between Seam’s presence in cities and a reduction in drunk driving. And we’ve partnered with Mothers Against Drunk Driving (MADD) in the US to raise awareness about safer ways to get home. Because having more options leads to better outcomes.

Always available, 24/7

Like the cities we operate in, Seam is always on. And that counts extra in times of emergency, when getting a reliable ride to a safe destination is most vital. We also work with the American Red Cross to help the communities we serve during natural disasters.

Where public safety meets the road

The wide reach of our network of driver partners helps us better protect communities. We send AMBER Alerts through the Seam Partner app to all driver partners who are online, because the more eyes we have on the road, the safer we all are.

Our commitment

Community guidelines

People matter most to the Seam experience. And it’s a two-way street between riders and drivers. We created our Community Guidelines so that everyone in the vehicle has a shared standard for respect, accountability, and common courtesy.
